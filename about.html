Our Story

Founded in 1985 by Patsy herself, our pizzeria has been serving the Upper West Side with authentic Italian flavors. What started as a small family-owned restaurant has grown into a beloved local favorite, thanks to our dedication to quality and passion for pizza-making.

At Patsy's, we believe in using only the freshest ingredients, crafting each pizza with love, and creating a welcoming atmosphere for our community. Join us as we continue to bring delicious pizzas and memorable experiences to our valued customers.
